# Product Vision — Find Your Job

> **Last updated**: 2026-06-11

---

## Vision Statement

Find Your Job is a **local-first job positioning platform** that helps tech talent understand job fit, prove skills through evaluations and certifications, and manage applications — while giving recruiters better signals before interviewing.

---

## Problem

Tech hiring has two connected problems:

1. **Candidates lack trusted feedback** about which jobs fit their skills, what gaps they have, and how to prove readiness.
2. **Recruiters lack reliable signals** before interviewing, creating noise, slow screening, and weak confidence in candidate quality.

---

## Solution

A platform that combines:
- Structured profiles with verifiable skills
- Applications with match context
- Evaluations and certifications as trust signals
- Weighted matching engine (skills 50%, evaluations 25%, experience 15%, certs 10%)
- Interview management for recruiters

---

## Target Users

| User | Primary Need |
|------|--------------|
| **Candidate** | Understand job fit, track applications, prove skills |
| **Recruiter** | Screen candidates efficiently, manage pipeline |
| **Admin** | Verify certifications, maintain trust boundaries |

---

## Value Proposition

### For Candidates
- See relevant jobs with match context
- Track applications and interviews in one place
- Build verifiable skill profile through evaluations and certifications

### For Recruiters
- Create and manage job postings
- Review applicants with match evidence
- Schedule and track interviews

### For the Market
- Reduce hiring noise through structured, verifiable signals
- Move beyond resume-only screening

---

## Key Differentiators

1. **Trust Model**: Three levels — Claimed (user-provided), Measured (activity-based), Verified (admin-confirmed)
2. **Match Transparency**: Scoring is explainable, not magic numbers
3. **Local-First**: No cloud dependency for MVP, runs on developer machine

---

## Non-Goals (Explicitly Excluded)

- Cloud SaaS deployment or multi-tenant billing
- Real payment processing
- Fully automated hiring decisions
- Replacing human recruiter judgment
- AI/NLP capabilities not implemented in backend

---

## Success Criteria

For MVP:
- Candidate can complete job search journey without dead ends
- Recruiter can create jobs, review applicants, manage interviews
- Match recommendations are visible and explainable
- Certifications can move from unverified to verified
- Demo flow works end-to-end with seed data
